Blockchain developer & consultant

Besides the thesis on Blockchain, the research carried out in this area and the prizes won for such research, I work in the Blockchain space as a developer and consultant since 2017.

In 2019 I worked for the startup Genuino Blockchain Technologies to create an NFT smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain to track products (in particular AC Fiorentina’s soccer shirts), a dashboard to interact with the smart contract (using React and ethers.js) and an application, for Android and iOS, to store ETH, GNT ERC-20 token, ERC-721 tokens associated to the products and a QR reader to see if a product is authentic or not.

Then in 2018 I mostly did private consultation to companies and startups which wanted to implement Blockchain technology inside their business. In particolar a Energy company that wanted to know how to use blockchain to create a decentralized marketplace for renewable energy and a startup that wanted to know how to use blockchain to track bikes.

Meanwhile, as a hobby, I write articles (mostly on Medium, HackerNoon and Linkedin) about blockchain tech (one of which was even retweeted by Jack Dorsey, the founder of Twitter). I was invited as a speaker at different conferences to speak about blockchain.

In 2017 I helped a startup (Epocum) to lunch its ICO by: developing an ERC-20 token and a crowdsale smart contract; writing the whitepaper and implementing web3.js to create a Dapp.
