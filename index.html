About me

Full stack web developer

I have always had to develop programs and web-apps on my own using different programming languages ​​and tools based on the need. For this reason over the years I have become very skilled with basic programming concepts and languages such as javascript, html, css, PHP and mysql.

Besides I worked on several projects as a freelancer, increasing my skills as a project manager and learning how to best use CMS like Wordpress and Prestashop.

In addition to being a developer, I have a strong passion for design, a passion that led me to learn very well Adobe Illustrator. Blockchain developer & consultant

This experience has led me to learn solidity and, therefore, how to program smart contracts and various types of tokens; how to use web3.js and ipfs; the differences between the various existing Blockchain protocols; how to make an ICO and list a token on the exchanges; ecc.

In addition to this experience, I collaborate with the advertising and consulting agency C O R P 13 7 as a Blockchain consultant.
